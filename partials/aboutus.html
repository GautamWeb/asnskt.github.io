Abhyuday S N Samaj kalyan Trust

Abhyuday S N Samaj Kalyan Trust was formed in the memory of Late Surya Narayan Pandey. He was an epitome of love,affection and values. He had a simple life and believed that eduction is the only salutation the social and economic development of the people and society. It was his dream to offer quality affordable education that transform the character of our children as they grew to become responsible citizen .Abhyuday S N Samaj Kalyan Trust is an independent globle charitable foundation dedicated to the skill development programme for girls,women & youth and give a education to children in the way to develop to our society.
